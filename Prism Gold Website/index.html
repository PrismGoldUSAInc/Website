Gold Purity Guide

Understanding gold carat ratings and purity levels

24K
99.99% Pure

24 Carat Gold

The purest form of gold available. Soft and malleable, perfect for investment purposes and premium jewelry.

  • Maximum gold content
  • Investment grade quality
  • Brilliant golden color
  • Highest resale value
23K
95.83% Pure

23 Carat Gold

High purity gold with minimal alloy content. Excellent for coins and premium jewelry with enhanced durability.

  • High gold content
  • Good durability
  • Rich golden appearance
  • Excellent investment choice
22K
91.7% Pure

22 Carat Gold

Traditional gold standard with added strength. Perfect balance of purity and durability for everyday jewelry.

  • Traditional purity level
  • Enhanced durability
  • Suitable for daily wear
  • Popular choice worldwide
